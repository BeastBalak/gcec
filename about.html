About GCEC

Global Center for Entrepreneurship (GCEC) integrates professional courses with undergraduate and postgraduate degree programmes. As a Section-8 non-profit, GCEC focuses on entrepreneurship, incubation and real-world exposure for students.

Infrastructure & Partners

Smart classrooms, innovation labs, tie-ups with AWS, Hubspot, IBM, Coursera, Github and others to support students and startups.
